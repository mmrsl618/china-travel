About Visit China Tips

Practical travel guides for your trip to China.

What We Do

Visit China Tips is a free, independent travel resource for international visitors planning a trip to China. We cover everything you need to know before you go — from visa applications and mobile payment setup to high-speed trains, local transport, and the best things to see and eat across the country.

Every guide is written with a single goal: make your trip to China smoother and more enjoyable. We focus on practical, actionable information that you can actually use.

What We Cover

  • Preparation — Visas, payments, VPN, essential apps, airport arrival
  • Getting Around — High-speed trains, flights, metro, taxis, ride-hailing, accommodation
  • Exploring China — City guides, itineraries, food guides, cultural tips

Our Approach

China travel information changes fast — payment systems evolve, visa policies update, new apps appear. We keep our guides up to date so you don't have to dig through forums and outdated blog posts.

This site is reader-supported. We may earn a commission from affiliate links, but all recommendations are based on firsthand experience and independent evaluation.
